Our Key Initiatives

Dummy data showing significant work across all sectors of our municipality.

Infrastructure

Upgraded 50km of rural roads, connecting 5 remote villages to the main market. Initiated the "Bright Palpa" project, installing 1,200 solar street lights.

Health & Sanitation

Established a new 15-bed health post in Ward 3. Launched a municipality-wide free health check-up camp, benefiting over 5,000 citizens.

Education

Implemented the "Smart Classroom" program in 10 community schools. Started a scholarship fund for underprivileged and talented students.

Tourism Promotion

Launched the "Palpa Heritage Trail," promoting local homestays. Hosted the first-ever Palpa Cultural Festival, attracting national tourism.

Agriculture

Distributed advanced irrigation tools to 500+ farmers. Started a new subsidy program for organic farming, increasing local organic produce by 40%.

E-Governance

Digitized all municipal records and launched an online portal for vital registrations, reducing wait times from 3 days to 30 minutes.
